WHAT IS MOBILE LEGENDS
BANG BANG

MLBB is a team-based strategy game where two teams of five powerful champions face off to destroy the other's base. Choose from over 120 Heroes to make epic play, secure kills, and take down towers as you battle your way to victory.

DESTROY THE BASE

The Base is the heart of both team's bases. Destroy the enemy's base first to win the game.
Your Base is where minions span. Behind your Base is the foundain, where you will spawn in the start of the game

CHOOSE YOUR LANE

There are five positions that make up the recommended team comp for the game. Each lane lends itself to certain kinds of heroes and roles—try them all or lock in to the lane that calls you.

EXP LANE

Also known as the offlaner, Exp laners are expected to gain much experience as possible to level up their abilities so that they can impact the game from the getgo.

JUNGLE

JUnglers are not just responsible for killing jungle monsters. They continously reotate the map to clear creeps and secure objectives, significantly impactingg the team's economy and standing.

MID LANE

Mid laner is the one role responsible for controlling the middle lane of the map. This lane is important because it leads to the river area that contains important objectives and access to other lanes.

GOLD LANE

This role focuses on dealing damage. As the name suggests, the primary objective of players in this role is to aquire gold as quickly as possible, allowing them to purchase items and scale well as the game progresses.

ROAMER

A roamer's duty is to roam and help allies, or just exploring the map to provide some visions around the river area. The roamer has the lowest priority in terms of getting resources in the game, such as gold and experience, because it does not need much items or levels to fulfill it's job and be effecitve.
